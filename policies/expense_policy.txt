Expense Reimbursement Policy
Version 2.1 – April 2024

1. Eligible Expenses
   - Travel (flights, train, rental car) for business purposes.
   - Meals up to $50/day (receipts required).
   - Office supplies under $100 (pre‑approval needed above $100).

2. Submission
   - Submit expenses within 30 days using the Concur system.
   - Attach all receipts (PDF or photo).

3. Approval
   - Expenses under $500 auto‑approved by manager.
   - Over $500 require finance department approval.

4. Reimbursement
   - Processed within 10 business days via direct deposit.
   - International expenses converted at company exchange rate.
