Remote Work Policy
Last Updated: March 15, 2024

1. Eligibility
   - Employees in good standing with at least 6 months tenure may apply for remote work.
   - Certain roles (e.g., security, facilities) are not eligible.

2. Schedule
   - Remote employees must work core hours 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in their time zone.
   - Full remote requires manager approval and a signed agreement.

3. Equipment
   - Company provides laptop, monitor, and necessary peripherals.
   - Internet reimbursement up to $50/month.

4. Communication
   - Must be available on Slack during working hours.
   - Attend all scheduled meetings via Zoom.

5. Termination
   - Remote work arrangement can be revoked with 2 weeks notice.
