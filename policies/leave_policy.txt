Company Leave Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. Annual Leave
   - Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid annual leave per year.
   - Leave must be requested at least 3 days in advance via the HR portal.

2. Sick Leave
   - Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year.
   - A doctor's note is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

3. Parental Leave
   - 12 weeks of paid parental leave for birth or adoption.
   - Must be taken within the first 12 months of the event.

4. Unpaid Leave
   - Unpaid leave may be granted for personal reasons, subject to manager approval.
   - Maximum 30 days per calendar year.

For questions, contact HR at hr@company.com.
