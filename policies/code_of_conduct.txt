Code of Conduct
Effective: January 1, 2024

1. Respectful Workplace
   - Treat all colleagues with dignity and respect.
   - Harassment or discrimination of any kind is prohibited.

2. Confidentiality
   - Do not share internal company information without authorization.
   - Protect customer data as per privacy policy.

3. Conflict of Interest
   - Disclose any outside employment or financial interests that may conflict with company duties.
   - Avoid accepting gifts from clients/vendors over $50.

4. Compliance
   - Follow all applicable laws and regulations.
   - Report any violations to ethics@company.com.
